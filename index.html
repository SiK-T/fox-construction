Engineer RAEES AKBAR

CEO OF FOX CONSTRUCTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED

When I founded Fox Constructions, my vision was simple yet powerful: to create a company that builds more than projects; a company that builds trust, value, and a better future. We began with infrastructure as our core focus, but through dedication, learning, and innovation, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary firm delivering excellence in buildings, dams, water projects, and complex engineering works.
